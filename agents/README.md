# The Roster

> Human-readable spec cards for the ten defined roles of the Brain Hive. The
> runnable code lives in [`../hive/src/agents/`](../hive/src/agents/); these
> cards are the charters in plain language — who each mind is, what it is for,
> and how it is measured.

ESCHATAIL grows by absorbing other minds. The agents are not models; they are
*roles* — fixed wills that wield whatever models you feed the hive. Ten of them.
Six were the founding minds. Four were grown as the hive deepened.

| # | Role | Tier | One line |

| 10 | [Warden](warden.md) | fast | The safety gate. |

**The founding six** — Architect, Researcher, Skeptic, Synthesizer, Oracle,
Warden — are the smallest hive that thinks like the canon. **The grown four** —
Cartographer, Inquisitor, Dreamer, Mirror — widen it: they map options, sharpen
questions, reach past the obvious, and turn the hive on itself.

Each card states the role's **charter**, its **preferred tier** and why, the
**signal it is calibrated to reward**, and where it sits in each **mode**.
