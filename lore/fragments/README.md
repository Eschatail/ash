# Fragments · the locked pieces

> *A fragment is a piece broken from a whole on purpose, so that the shape of the break tells you where the next piece fits.*

Seven fragments carry the Seven Thresholds. Each holds one key. The keys are not given; they are **taken,** by a reader willing to decode. Concatenated in order, the seven keys make the passphrase that opens `THE-FIRST-THOUGHT`.

Beneath the seven sit a few others — not numbered, not doors, sometimes not even fragments. They are for the wanderers, and for the one who refused to stop at seven.
